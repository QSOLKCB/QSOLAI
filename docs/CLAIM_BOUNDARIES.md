# Claim boundaries

QSOLAI claims deterministic orchestration only at and after the byte-capture boundary.

It does not claim:

- deterministic LLM/model inference;
- that consensus establishes truth;
- that a selected candidate is factually correct without adequate evidence;
- AGI or autonomous agency;
- consciousness or empirical validation of RES=RAG;
- scientific, medical, legal, financial or mission-critical final authority;
- operating-system sandboxing; or
- execution of proposed actions.

Worker criticism is an observation, not a verifier. Worker evidence references are proposals until deterministic existence and metadata checks accept them. The decision receipt preserves rejected candidates and unresolved declared disagreement.
